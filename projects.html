2024 Infy App Design Challenge

By: Shifa, Emily, Afrin, & Srutha

CollieAssist is an app designed to support individuals, including college applicants, by providing assistance with managing daily tasks and alleviating stress during the complex process of college applications. For students, CollieAssist offers tools to organize college application requirements, track deadlines, and receive helpful reminders, which can reduce the overwhelming nature of the application process. By providing structured support, such as information on application timelines, essay prompts, and other requirements, the app aims to relieve stress and empower students to stay on top of their tasks with confidence. This focus on organization and personalized guidance makes CollieAssist a valuable resource for college-bound students navigating this high-stakes period.

MFTA Warehouse Reuse Centers Nationwide Brochure

For my brochure project at the MFTA warehouse, my fellow intern through the Bloomberg Arts Internship and I conducted in-depth research on reuse centers across the U.S. I gathered information on the different types of centers in each state, creating a comprehensive guide for MFTA to know where to donate materials they couldn’t use. The goal was to support MFTA’s sustainability mission by helping them efficiently redirect surplus materials to other centers that share similar goals. This project allowed me to contribute to MFTA’s operations practically, ensuring unused supplies could find new life in communities that would benefit from them.
